## Usability Issues Addressed

| # | Issue | Original Problem | Solution Implemented | Heuristic |
|---|-------|------------------|---------------------|-----------|
| 1 | Oversized Logo | 250px height wasted space | Reduced to 70px compact header | Aesthetic & Minimalist |
| 2 | Poor Contact Visibility | Tiny text, hard to find | Prominent in header, 14px | Visibility of Status |
| 3 | No Clear CTA | Users unclear on next step | Yellow "View Products" button | User Control |
| 4 | Text Overload | 200+ word paragraphs | Card-based, scannable layout | Minimalist Design |
| 5 | Broken Social Feed | Unprofessional appearance | Removed, clean footer | Error Recognition |
| 6 | Navigation Unclear | No active states | Clear hierarchy, active states | Consistency |
| 7 | No Mobile View | Fixed-width layout | Responsive considerations | Flexibility |
| 8 | Poor Visual Hierarchy | Inconsistent design | Systematic color/typography | Standards |
